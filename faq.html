Frequently Asked Questions

General Ordering Information
1. How does our custom writing service work?
To learn about how our service works, please visit the “How It Works” page where you can read a detailed explanation of our features, along with a guide of steps to get your paper completed
2. How do I fill in the order form?
There are two types of order forms: a short one and a more extended one. The first one is located on the main page of our website. Once you fill out a short order form in the right corner of our website's main page, click the “Continue” button to move on to a more extended form. At this stage, if you do not have an account with EssayGuru.com, it will automatically be created by our system, and a password will be sent to your e-mail immediately. Please note that later, once you place your order and enter your personal account, you will be able to reset this password to any other combination of signs and numbers. Next, you are redirected to a more extended order form, where you can select a deadline, upload any additional materials for the writer, and specify other important requirements for your paper. To learn more about how to place an order with EssayGuru.com, you can visit our “How It Works” page on the website.
How will I be able to send my case study/articles/materials to the writer?

You are able to upload any additional materials for the writer, be it a draft, an outline, or some reading material, at the stage of filling out your order form.

What is the format of your custom essays?

There are several formatting styles you can choose from when placing an order (MLA, APA, Chicago / Turabian or Not applicable), and you will be getting a paper properly formatted in accordance with the rules of this particular style. In case you do not need any heading, title page, or references formatted (for example, if you are ordering a resume), you should choose the “ Not applicable” option, and the paper will be presented in a plain MS Word document.

What if I forgot my password?

If you forgot your password, please click on the link Forgot Password, which is located below the “Login” button on the website's login page.

Order Completion
When will my paper be done?

When you initially fill out the order form, you select the deadline by which you need the paper completed The paper will be completed by this stated deadline. However, please note that if your deadline has changed to a shorter one, we strongly encourage you to communicate in advance.

How can I modify my initial order requirements?

Once you have published your order and the writers have started applying for it, you can still change your order details, such as the number of pages, the title, or the instructions for your paper. In order to do so, you can click on the “Edit order details” button on your order page. However, please note that in case the deadline, the number of pages, or the title of the order has been modified, all writers' application will automatically be considered outdated.

If you would like to modify instructions at a later stage, when the writer was already assigned to work on your order, you will have to communicate and discuss any changes to your initial order instructions that you wish to make. If the modifications significantly differ from the initial instructions, then you might be required to place a new order.

What if I do not like my essay?

If you do not like what the writer has written, we recommend you to communicate your comments with no hesitation, while the order is still in progress, so that the writer does not have to start a new later on.

You can instantly let the writer know whether he or she is doing something wrong or guide him or her in a different direction. However, once the order is fully completed and the full amount has been paid the order is automatically set on “Finished” status. What you can do in such a case is place an order for editing or rewriting

Writer Information
Can I be sure that all writers listed on the website have passed an evaluation?

Every writer has to go through a very strict evaluation and therefore all writers working for EssayGuru.com are committed to high working standards and are also well-educated. They have several years of relevant professional experience in a particular field of study. Moreover, each writer is often supervised by the writing department at the company to ensure that the high standard is maintained. Moreover, with our transparent rating system, our customers' feedback serves as the best indicator of each writer's performance. There are different criteria that can therefore be used when choosing a writer • writer's rating and order history with feedback from previous customers • writer's style and approach to working on the first page of your order

Writer Information
Can I be sure that all writers listed on the website have passed an evaluation?

The rating system for the writers was designed to create transparency and also to ensure that there is competition among the writers therefore resulting in higher quality of work. The writers’rating for each writer is calculated automatically based and it is based on the votes of every customer who worked with this writer in the past and rated this writer's level of cooperation towards an order's completion.

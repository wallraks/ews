About Us

This company has worked in academic assistance with great success for over 10 years. We are always looking for better ways to improve the quality of the services that we provide and also to increase customer satisfaction.

We have an innovative concept due to the success we have had. ews.co.ke is the result of the working experience as well as the customer feedback we have had. Our market resources have also led to the creation of this unique online service.

ews.co.ke is a unique on-line platform in that it offers academic assistance services with utmost emphasis on top-quality product. Our platform facilitates development of a mutually-satisfying final product. The advantages of this set up are: the consumers are in a position to keep track of their orders from the beginning to the end.

From the experience of working in the academic assistance field for a long period of time, it is clear that the customer is the best person to manage their orders.

With ews.co.ke:

  • Customers can monitor and supervise as the writers work on the orders, and help the writers improve their work so that they can both get full satisfaction.
  • Customers are given high-quality products at affordable prices.

Furthermore, our customers have to be fully content so the writer has to fully meet their needs and work is done satisfactorily. This approach is innovative as it raises standard of the work done. Our writers go through the instructions and the deadline and then determine whether the job is suitable. It is then charged based on the complexity and the deadline. The customer has a big impact on the quality of work done. The instructions given determine the quality of work. This allows the writer to be able to produce the best possible quality product. All in all, this innovative platform offers a unique and simple platform that is very convenient. It helps customers who may be struggling with their academics and gives professional writers the chance to use their skills to help the customers succeed in their studies. The mission at ews.co.ke is to ensure that both sides are content with the end product. It is our belief that no other company has a better solution in the market.
